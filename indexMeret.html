    the rules:
  • each player choose a square to put his sign in it (X or O).
  • the X always begins.
  • there are three ways to win the game:
  • the first to put his / her sign along the whole row wins.
  • the first to put his / her sign along the whole column wins.
  • the first to put his / her sign along the whole diagonal wins.
  • if no one won and theboard is full then it is a tie.
